## 🔬 Research Interests

- **AI-driven constitutive models** — Physics-informed deep learning for fiber-reinforced polymer nanocomposites
- **Phase-field fracture modeling** — Finite deformation models under hygrothermal conditions
- **Multiscale modeling** — From molecular dynamics to continuum mechanics
- **Computational mechanics** — Nonlinear FEM, nonlocal continuum damage mechanics
- **Atomistic simulations** — Molecular dynamics, coarse-grained models, LAMMPS
- **Polymer nanocomposites** — Wind turbine rotor blades, self-healing composites
